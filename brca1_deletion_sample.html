Population & Disease Context

Gene function: BRCA1 encodes a tumor suppressor protein critical for DNA double-strand break repair via homologous recombination. Germline loss-of-function variants confer high lifetime risk of breast and ovarian cancer.

Known mechanism: Loss of heterozygosity (LOH) is a critical second hit in BRCA1-associated tumorigenesis. Chromosomal deletions affecting 17q (BRCA1 locus) are among the earliest somatic events in breast and ovarian cancers in germline carriers.
